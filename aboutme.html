The Journey of my life

I am writing to you regarding a one year Web Development Course at your institute. I am extremely interested in having the opportunity to join your Institute for a year long boot camp, as my education background and previous work experiences prepares me to further start with the It career. Over the past five years in Berlin, I have obtained my Bachelor degree in International Business. At the university we were taught many core business concepts but the IT part was very little to negligible.
However, the current job market has shifted to mostly online marketing and It related jobs. Therefore, I was always looking for some sort of depth knowledge which Universities do not seem to offer. Later on, I started learning myself on codecademy and youtube channels. There I explored how much interested I was in coding. In my free time I would always code and try to make websites. So I believe my immense passion towards Web Development, my curiosity of creating new things makes me a good candidate for the one year Web Development course.
Four week orientation course, in my opinion, was a very important step towards giving students the opportunity to explore various aspects of the trending marketing and Web development spectrum. Although I am opting for Web Development, the online marketing classes were very fascinating. I learnt there SEO,SEA, Analytics and so much more, that previously i thought was not so appealing. After the orientation course, I am massively convinced how both courses are interconnected and go head to head together. My motivation for one year course is very simple. I want to become a Web Developer, whereby I can create amazing websites. It makes me happy to see what I have created. This is why I have chosen this career path over some call center jobs or gastronomy jobs.
